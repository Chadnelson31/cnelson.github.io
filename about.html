Let's share some technology

Technology in general is one of our greatest assets in modern day society. It's monumental influence can be seen from city to city and person to person. With great technology comes much waste though.

Yearly, old tech is one of the main contributors to waste globally. My goal as a developer is to find new and creative ways to use and share old technologies to their greatest advantages.

So what do you say, let's create something great shall we?
